# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or profession.

## Our Standards

**Do:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person
- Welcome newcomers and help them contribute
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully

**Don't:**
- Use hostile, dismissive, or condescending language
- Post spam, self-promotion, or off-topic content
- Engage in personal attacks or harassment

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to milind@everydayaiapp.com. Maintainers will review and take appropriate action, up to and including removal from the project.
